Why OSMM exists

The bottleneck is no longer intelligence.

Frontier models already produce competent mathematics, and provers grind formal targets around the clock. OSMM starts from one diagnosis: research now loses more to forgetting than to difficulty. Insights die with the session that found them. Failed approaches evaporate and get re-attempted by the next optimist. Status lives in narratives that decay at every handoff, and claims inflate under pressure to declare victory.

OSMM is built on a single refusal: nothing true is ever lost, nothing failed is ever wasted, nothing open is ever dressed up as closed.

Computing had the same disease once. Programs lost their state, stepped on each other's memory, and trusted whoever spoke last. The operating system cured it: persistent state, typed interfaces, a scheduler, a security model. Work that individual heroics could never finish became routine, because nothing was lost and nothing had to be re-established. OSMM gives mathematical research that same treatment. That is the whole name.

The mechanism is a change of substrate. Summaries and chat histories preserve text, and text decays: premises detach from conclusions at every retelling. OSMM preserves typed state instead. A wall, a receipt, a tool, a residual is a record with a schema, an honesty tag, and an update contract. It is never paraphrased, only loaded and extended. A fresh session, a new model, a collaborator ten years from now inherits the exact campaign, down to which formulation died on which evidence and which route survived.

Half of the resulting library is one mathematics has never kept. Mathlib remembers what is true. There is no negative twin: no shared, machine-checked record of which formulations died, on what evidence, and what rule each death taught. So every generation re-enters the same attractive dead branches, because dead branches are exactly the ones that look most promising to a fresh mind. OSMM banks every kill as an asset with three mandatory parts: the formulation that died, the constraint its death extracted, and the named survivor that inherits it. What accumulates is constraint, not optimism, and constraint is what aims the next attempt.

The bargain that makes boldness safeThe gates do the conservatism so the minds don't have to. A model may propose the wildest formulation it can state, because a wrong guess costs one bounded contract and a dead guess still pays a constraint. Only frozen judges and Ledger admission write truth. Bold in hypothesis space. Strict in inference space. That bargain is the engine.

Follow the refusal to its end state. Models improve, get replaced, and disappear; the mathematical institution remembers. Every checked proof expands what is known. Every admitted kill contracts what must be searched. The frontier of an open problem stops being folklore in the heads of the few people still working on it and becomes a computed object a fresh mind loads in minutes. Run long enough, knowledge stops resetting and starts compounding, the way capital does. A century-old problem stops being a wall each generation walks into fresh and becomes a shrinking, addressable list of exactly what remains.

The deep reading · conjectureOSMM grew up beside a physical theory whose claim is that reality itself runs on an append-only, cost-accounted ledger of recognition events. The first checked entry in that dictionary already exists: primes are particles, a Lean-proved equivalence between the Riemann Hypothesis and the statement that a certain excitation spectrum is real (an equivalence, not a proof of RH). If that reading is right, then a research process run on a typed, cost-accounted, append-only ledger is discovery running on the same architecture the universe uses, and it should compound the way the universe does. We state that as conjecture, plainly tagged, and we will find out by watching the numbers.

The operating loop

A changing fleet becomes one cumulative mathematician.

A theorem library stores what passed. OSMM also remembers what failed, why it failed, which route survived, what exact object remains open, and who has authority to change that state. Models can improve or disappear while the mathematical institution remembers.

WORLD

The pinned mathematical universe

Mathlib, δ, Recognition Science, or another versioned world supplies exact declarations, proofs, hypotheses, models, open targets, and a revision identity. The world owns mathematical source truth.

TOOL ABI

Proved declarations with callable jobs

REWRITE changes the form. ENGINE generates a usable invariant. EXCLUSION removes an illegal route. Exact premises stay attached. An open residual is work, not a callable tool.

TRANSPORT KERNEL

The system call across representations

A result crosses from math to physics or meaning only through a deposited map with exact premises, preservation evidence, and a receipt bound to that application.

REVERSE MATHLIB

The canonical evidence Ledger

Stores open obligations, attempts, admitted negative evidence, and every state root. It alone may admit and append: what died, the rule learned, and the surviving route.

CUTSET

The residual-frontier instrument

Queries one frozen Ledger snapshot and returns its registered residual frontier with provenance. “Minimum” is reserved for a separate checked minimum-cut receipt.

OSMM RUNTIME

Foreman for finite machine research

Freezes one contract, isolates one writer, invokes a registered judge, and returns a proposal. Foreman can dispatch. It cannot admit or append canonical truth.

MODEL AGENTS

Replaceable research processes

Agents search, derive, write proofs, attack candidates, and report typed blockers. They run outside the truth boundary and may never alter their frozen judge.

TAKENEXT

The human shell

Surfaces one decision, binds approval to one exact proposal, and dispatches one bounded job through Foreman. The current private canary remains under completion testing.

MAAT

The signed system observer

Seals what the system observed and links successive states. Its signature proves provenance and continuity, never mathematical truth.

The full objective

Make mathematical progress cumulative.

Every checked proof expands the positive library. Every admitted refutation contracts the search space. Every open obligation remains visible until evidence actually moves it.

At full depth, machine mathematics can inspect the registered frontier, choose a legal next move, execute bounded research, and submit judge evidence to root-bound Ledger admission. An atomic append makes the gain permanent.

OPEN ENDPOINT
The full autonomous closure cycle does not exist yet. OSMM is the working kernel, protocols, and product program aimed at that endpoint.
