Why OSMM exists

Mathematics that cannot touch the world stays unfinished.

A proved theorem can sit forever as text. A physical claim can sit forever as metaphor. OSMM exists to close that gap with machinery: forced mathematical structure, a grammar that makes math→physics→meaning translations checkable, theorems typed as callable jobs, and installable correspondence worlds where those jobs can run.

In its final form, OSMM is the operating system that makes mathematics programmatic in physical reality: pure mathematical necessity forced into checkable instruments that compound across domains until open residuals shrink under evidence.

The spine is fixed. δ supplies the actual mathematics. δ forced is the boundary where that mathematics becomes physically applicable only through named correspondence. Under that boundary sit three instruments: Physicality Grammar (deposit-only translations with nouns, verbs, receipts, and squares), Theorem Tools (REWRITE, TRANSPORT, ENGINE, EXCLUSION, RESIDUAL), and Cutset (the residual viewer that banks kills and prints what still remains). Recognition Science Lean is the first rich installed correspondence world, with seeds such as primes-are-particles (an equivalence, not a proof of RH).

What makes this an operating system rather than a library is authority and composition. Worlds pin source truth. Tools are typed syscalls with exact premises. Grammar admits a crossing only when a deposited square clears. The Ledger alone appends status. Lean and lake alone write THEOREM. Empirical receipts are a separate certificate class. Runtime may propose; it may never admit.

Final instantiation · tagged honestlyEXISTS NOW (MODEL / MEASURED prototypes): δ world, early Cutset bank, first grammar squares, theorem-tool wrappers, RS correspondence seeds. BUILDABLE NEXT: a runtime that loads a goal, calls typed tools, grows grammar only by deposits, and keeps dual certificates. FINAL STATE (OPEN / CONJECTURE): install new correspondence worlds, run cross-domain campaigns that transport theorems into physics and other domains through grammar, leave permanent receipts, and shrink open problems to empty residuals under forced δ. Cross-domain compounding stays OPEN until a second independent world reuses the same interfaces without kernel changes.

Cutset answers one question inside that machine: what remains? Its tagline, “Write down every kill so you never forget it,” is the instrument’s vow, not OSMM’s identity. Lead with forced math made physical. Put Cutset where it belongs: under δ forced, beside Grammar and Theorem Tools.

Worlds, tools, grammar, ledger, judges.

A theorem library stores what passed. OSMM also types what a theorem can do, checks translations into physics and meaning, installs correspondence worlds, and admits status only through frozen judges. Models can improve or disappear while the institution keeps dual certificates: Lean theorems and empirical receipts stay separate.

WORLD

The pinned mathematical universe

Mathlib, δ, Recognition Science, or another versioned world supplies exact declarations, proofs, hypotheses, models, open targets, and a revision identity. The world owns mathematical source truth.

TOOL ABI

Proved declarations with callable jobs

REWRITE changes the form. ENGINE generates a usable invariant. EXCLUSION removes an illegal route. Exact premises stay attached. An open residual is work, not a callable tool.

TRANSPORT KERNEL

The system call across representations

A result crosses from math to physics or meaning only through a deposited map with exact premises, preservation evidence, and a receipt bound to that application.

REVERSE MATHLIB

Stores open obligations, attempts, admitted negative evidence, and every state root. It alone may admit and append: what died, the rule learned, and the surviving route.

CUTSET

Query-only residual instrument

Reads one frozen Ledger snapshot and returns its registered residual frontier with provenance. Cutset never owns memory and never admits status. “Minimum” needs a separate checked receipt.

OSMM RUNTIME

Foreman for finite machine research

MODEL AGENTS

Agents search, derive, write proofs, attack candidates, and report typed blockers. They run outside the truth boundary and may never alter their frozen judge.

TAKENEXT

Surfaces one decision, binds approval to one exact proposal, and dispatches one bounded job through Foreman. The current private canary remains under completion testing.

MAAT

The signed system observer

Seals what the system observed and links successive states. Its signature proves provenance and continuity, never mathematical truth.

The full objective

Make mathematics executable across reality.

Every checked proof becomes a typed tool. Every deposited grammar square makes a translation checkable. Every admitted result leaves a permanent receipt. Every open obligation stays visible until evidence actually moves it.

At full depth, a human or machine can install a correspondence world, compose theorem tools through Physicality Grammar, run a bounded campaign under frozen judges, and shrink the residual frontier. Cross-domain compounding of that kind remains OPEN until a second independent world reuses the same interfaces without kernel changes.
