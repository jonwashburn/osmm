The operating loop

A research campaign becomes durable state.

A theorem library stores what passed. OSMM also records scoped negative evidence, typed omissions, current obligations, and the receipt that authorized each status change. The campaign can move between people and machines without collapsing into chat history.

INPUTFormal library

Definitions, theorems, exact hypotheses, and named goals.

MEMORYCampaign ledger

Events, attempts, admitted walls, debts, and provenance.

FRONTIERResidual state

The open leaves still blocking the goal in the current registry.

ACTIONBounded contracts

Fixed work, one writer, one gate, and a typed terminal.

REVERSE MATHLIB

The negative library

Stores admitted negative evidence beside positive theorems: the formulation excluded, the constraint learned, and the surviving route that inherits it.

CUTSET

The residual-frontier instrument

Composes registered proofs, scoped walls, and open obligations into the current residual frontier. The output is relative to its registry and carries provenance.

The full objective

Make mathematical progress cumulative.

Every checked proof expands the positive library. Every admitted refutation contracts the search space. Every open obligation remains visible until evidence actually moves it.

At full depth, machine mathematics can inspect the registered frontier, choose a legal next move, execute bounded research, and deposit a checker-approved result back into shared state. The system then remembers the gain permanently.

OPEN ENDPOINT
The full autonomous closure cycle does not exist yet. OSMM is the working kernel, protocols, and product program aimed at that endpoint.
